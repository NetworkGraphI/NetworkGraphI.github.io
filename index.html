NetworkGraphI

Research project that aims to discover the hidden connections between the people and study the community

Description

We're studying the conncetions between the people by performing Network analysis to discover patterns made by the people themselves, hidden connections between nodes, and the similarity between communities.
The project consists of network analysis measurements too such as degree of distribution, degree of centrality, and community discovery

Structural

Overall structure of a social network, such as the degree distribution, clustering coefficient, and path length

Pattern

The content of the interactions social network, such as the topics discussed, the sentiment expressed, or the language used.

Community

Identifying groups of nodes within a social network that have stronger connections to each other than to other nodes in the network.
